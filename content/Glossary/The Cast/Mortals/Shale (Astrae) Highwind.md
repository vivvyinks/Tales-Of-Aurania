The Rose Sword of Shell Grove and the runner for the [[Mirage]]. Shale is quick on his feet and quick with answers for most questions. He places a lot of weight on his friendships, and will go out of his way to ensure that no one is left behind. 

Originally from the wilds, Shale migrated to Elpis Renatus to seek his fortune before he became a crewmember of the Mirage.
